Privacy Policy

Tidbits Trivia · Last updated June 17, 2026

Tidbits Trivia is built to respect you. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Tidbits has no account, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. We do not know who you are and we never see how you play.

What stays on your device

Your scores, streak, and game progress are saved only on your own device (in the app's local storage). They are never uploaded. Deleting the app removes them.

Network use

The full question bank ships inside the app and works offline. The only time Tidbits uses the network is when you choose to "Create a quiz" from a topic — it then requests public articles from Wikipedia's API to build that quiz. These requests contain no personal information and are not associated with you.

Children

Tidbits is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will appear on this page with a new date.
