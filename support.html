Tidbits Support

Tidbits turns the whole of Wikipedia into a trivia game. Here's help.

Get in touch

Email ben@learningischange.com — questions, bug reports, or a question that looks wrong (include the topic so we can fix it).

Common questions

Do I need an account or internet?

No. Tidbits works fully offline and has no account. The only feature that uses the internet is "Create a quiz" from a topic.

Where do the questions come from?

They're generated from Wikipedia and Wikidata — real, sourced facts. Every question ends with the fact and a link to read more.

A question looks wrong. What do I do?

Email us the topic and what looked off. Because questions are built from live encyclopedic data, occasional edge cases slip through and we fix them.

Is my streak saved if I switch devices?

In v1, progress is stored on each device separately. Cross-device sync is coming in a later update.
